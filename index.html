Word Blitzen

Head-to-Head Word Puzzle

Engage in lightning-fast word battles against friends and opponents worldwide in this addictive puzzle game! Race against the clock and your opponent to claim letters and complete words first in this fast-paced challenge.

Game Modes

Online Match

Connect through Game Center to compete head-to-head against players worldwide. Enjoy real-time 60-second battles with new words from random categories every game.

Practice Mode

Hone your skills against the Blitzen Trainer with three difficulty levels:

  • Easy: Perfect for beginners to learn the game mechanics.
  • Medium: A balanced challenge for regular players.
  • Hard: Test your skills against a lightning-fast opponent.

How to Play

Each game features five words with their first letters revealed. Tap letters from the circular board to complete the words as quickly as possible.

  • Claim letters by tapping them on the board.
  • Complete words to earn bonus points.
  • Increase your score with each successful letter placement.
  • Beat your opponent before the 60-second timer runs out.

Features

  • Intuitive Controls: Simple tap-to-select gameplay accessible to all ages.
  • Dynamic Scoring: Earn points for each letter and bonus points for completing words.
  • Game Center Integration: Compete on global leaderboards and track your stats.
  • Player Statistics: View your performance statistics.
  • Diverse Word Categories: Encounter words from various categories.
  • Cross-Device Syncing: Your progress follows you across all your iOS devices.
  • Real-Time Multiplayer: Instant matchmaking with players around the world.
  • Sleek Design: Modern interface with smooth animations.

Unique Game Elements

  • Strategic Depth: Leverage your opponent's revealed letters to complete your words faster.
  • Speed Rewards: Swift moves can secure multiple instances of the same letter in one move.
  • Penalty System: Incorrect selections incur a time penalty, giving your opponent an edge.
  • Visual Feedback: Dynamic, color-coded indicators show letter ownership.
