Privacy Policy

This page explains how Hot Potato Apps handles information across its mobile games and related services. Some features only apply when a specific game uses them, such as multiplayer, leaderboards, ads, or in-app purchases.

Effective Date: March 11, 2025

Information We May Collect

Depending on the app and the features you use, we may receive:

  • basic device, crash, and performance information
  • game progress, scores, achievements, and leaderboard activity
  • Game Center details if you use Apple multiplayer or leaderboards
  • purchase status for items such as ad removal
  • ad-related and analytics signals used by supported third-party services

How We Use Information

  • to run game features like saves, leaderboards, multiplayer, and progression
  • to improve stability, fix bugs, and understand overall app performance
  • to support ads and monetization features where enabled
  • to process and restore eligible in-app purchases
  • to maintain the security and integrity of our apps and services

Third-Party Services

Our apps may rely on third-party providers. These services operate under their own privacy terms and may handle data according to their own policies.

Apple Game Center Leaderboards, multiplayer, achievements, and related profile data. Apple Privacy Policy
Google Firebase Analytics, backend support, and performance or diagnostic tooling. Google Privacy Policy
Google AdMob Advertising and related measurement when ad-supported features are enabled. Google Privacy Policy
Apple In-App Purchase Purchase processing, entitlement checks, and restoration of eligible purchases. Apple Privacy Policy

Data Retention and Security

We keep information only as long as reasonably needed for app functionality, legal compliance, fraud prevention, support, or service improvement. We also use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, but no system can promise absolute security.

Your Choices

You can manage permissions and many privacy-related settings through your device, platform account, or the app itself. Depending on the service involved, you may also be able to manage Game Center, ad, or analytics preferences directly with Apple or Google.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in a way that violates applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal information improperly, please contact us so we can review and address the issue.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date on this page. Continued use of our apps after an update means the revised policy applies.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Hot Potato Apps through the website linked from our app pages or our App Store developer page.
