Privacy Policy for Pinky Pile

Introduction

Pinky Pile is a physics-based stacking game that includes gameplay progress, best times, Game Center leaderboard support, optional sharing features, and advertising provided through Google AdMob.

  • Performance Data: We may collect information about how the App performs, including crash information, app errors, and other diagnostic data.
  • Advertising Data: When ads are shown through Google AdMob, AdMob may collect and process technical and usage information related to ad delivery, performance, and measurement.

Information You Provide

When you use certain features of the App, you may provide information that includes:

  • Game Center Information: When you connect to Apple's Game Center, we may receive your Game Center player ID and display name to support authentication, leaderboards, and score submission.
  • Shared Content: If you choose to use the App's sharing features, the App may generate screenshots, videos, or share text that you choose to send through your device's sharing tools.

We use the information we collect to:
